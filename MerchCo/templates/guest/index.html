About Us

MerchCO is an online website exclusively for the active members of Merchant Association of Kerala which provide financial assistance to its members.

We Solve Your Financial Problem

Welcome to the Merchant Association Group, a dedicated platform offering support and financial security to its members. Our organization provides accessible loans to help businesses grow and thrive, ensuring that members have the resources they need to succeed. In addition, we offer a welfare program to provide assistance to the families of members in the unfortunate event of their death, giving peace of mind to those who rely on our community. By joining our association, members gain access to vital financial services and a strong support network, promoting both business success and personal well-being.

How It Works

To get the most out of our website, you must complete these instructions.

01. Registration

It is expected that you are already a member of the Merchants Union. First, you must create an account on the website by entering the necessary details.

Learn More

02. Get an approval

Following registration, you may be approved by the Admin. Then you will be alloted to a unit according to yyour location. After receiving approval, you can use your username and password to access our website.

Learn More

03. Financial Transactions

You can either apply for loans or deposite your savings after logging into the website. Within the allotted time, you should make interest-bearing installment payments on the loan. Failure to pay could result in legal action and license cancellation.

Learn More

04. Welfares

offers a welfare program that uses funds collected from corresponding unit members to help members' relatives in the sad event that they pass away.

Learn More

05. Notifications

Notifications are provided in the event of an emergency. Additionally, alerts are sent out to remind them of the payments that are due, along with a link to make the payment.

Learn More

06. Dues

Welfare payments and loan installments are added to your debt if you don't make them. If the debt exceeds the deposit, you will be blocked from the website.
